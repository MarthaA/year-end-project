To our dearest founders and friends:

Thank you for being part of another eventful year at Bessemer Venture Partners! As we kick off 2018, here’s a quick look at why 2017 was a year to remember.

Our Letter

01/04

Our Partnership Grew

In June, we were delighted to promote three all-star investors to partner: Amit Karp in Israel, Charles Birnbaum in New York, and Kristina Shen in San Francisco. We are now 16 partners strong, across five global offices, supporting the visions of founders around the world.

02/04

Our Portfolio (Happily) Expanded

We are thrilled to welcome 27 new companies from the U.S., Israel, and India to the Bessemer family, which now numbers 190 companies. We are in awe of our companies’ tireless work ethic and countless achievements.

04/04

Our Anti-Portfolio Grew, (Regrettably)

You may have heard of the three companies that we regrettably added to our Anti-Portfolio this year: Atlassian, Airbnb and Snapchat. We don’t always get it right. Do we at least get points for humility?

03/04

Our Companies Hit Major Milestones

We were extremely proud to see 13 of our companies exit this year. Allena Pharmaceuticals, Blue Apron, Matrimony.com and SendGrid went public, bringing our total IPO count to 121. Congratulations to these teams for reaching this impressive achievement!

We applaud all the hard work and long hours that went into building these great companies (and we really appreciate the bragging rights they give us at parties.)

We were also excited to see nine of our portfolio companies acquired, including MindMeld (Cisco), Intacct (Sage Software), LifeLock (Symantec), nToggle (Rubicon), and Nominum (Akamai).

Wishing everyone a wonderful 2018!

Thank you to each of you for being a part of our journey in 2017. As we head into 2018, we continue to invest in exceptional consumer, enterprise, and healthcare founders. Here’s to another great year!

Sincerely,

Bessemer Venture Partners
